WhereCOVID-19 Data Summary

  • 1. Illinois Data

    COVID-19 data from the Illinois Department of Public Health (IDPH)

    • County-level data (cumulative, in JSON format) with attributes of county name, confirmed cases, total tested cases, and demographic breakdown by age
      source: http://www.dph.illinois.gov/sitefiles/CountyDemos.json?nocache=1
    • Zip-code-level data (cumulative, in JSON format) with attributes of zip code, confirmed cases, total tested cases, and demographic breakdown by age
      source: http://www.dph.illinois.gov/sitefiles/COVIDZip.json?nocache=1
    • Historical testing results
      • Cumulative characteristics of counties; (county, confirmed_cases, total_tested, deaths, latitude, and longitude)
      • State-level testing results by date (testDate, total_tested, confirmed cases, and deaths)
      • State-level demographics by age (age_group, count, tested, deaths, and demographics_by_race)
      • State-level demographics by race (race_description, count, tested, and deaths)
      • State-level recovery data (updated weekly) (report_date, recovered_cases, recovered_and_deceased_cases, and recovery_rates)
      • Historical daily characteristics of counties (county, confirmed_cases, total_tested, deaths, latitude, and longitude)
      source: http://www.dph.illinois.gov/sitefiles/COVIDHistoricalTestResults.json?nocache=1


    Other datasets

    • Social Vulnerability Index from CDC
      source: https://svi.cdc.gov/data-and-tools-download.html
    • HIV Carrier Density: AIDSVu
      source: https://aidsvu.org/
    • Spatial accessibility measurement from Kang et al. (2020)
      source: https://medrxiv.org/cgi/content/short/2020.05.06.20093534v1

  • 2. United States

    Data for all of the US states and counties provided by New York Times which is updated daily

    • US state-level data including attributes of date, state, fips, cases, and deaths
      source: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nytimes/covid-19-data/master/us-states.csv
    • US county-level data including attibutes of date, county, state, fips, cases, and deaths
      source: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nytimes/covid-19-data/master/us-counties.csv

  • 3. World

    Data provided by the World Health Organization

    • Global data updated daily with attributes of day, country, region, and metrics (deaths, cumulative deaths, confirmed, and cumulative confirmed) including historical data
      source: https://dashboards-dev.sprinklr.com/data/9043/global-covid19-who-gis.json
